# Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📊 Overview
An interactive Power BI Dashboard developed to analyze sales performance, track key business metrics (KPIs), and deliver actionable business insights using the sales financials dataset.

## 🛠️ Key Features & Layout
- **Top Section (KPI Cards):** High-level overview of Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Units Sold, and Profit Margin %.
- **Interactive Slicers:** Clean dropdown filters for Country, Segment, and Discount Category to streamline user navigation.
- **Trend Analysis:** Monthly Sales Trend using dynamic line charts to track seasonal peak periods.
- **Visual Segments:** Sales Distribution by Country (Bubble Map), Sales by Customer Segment, Units Sold by Product (Treemap), and Top Products by Revenue.

## 💡 Business Insights Generated
1. **Top Revenue Driver:** Paseo contributes the highest revenue among all products.
2. **Volume Leader:** The Government segment generates the largest sales volume.
3. **Seasonality:** Sales peak significantly during Q4.
4. **Profitability:** Overall profit margin remains healthy and consistent above 13%.
